## Speed Outcome

The accepted pre-refresh controlled comparisons showed a net throughput win, not
an across-the-board speedup. The final pre-refresh comparison (`T070`) passed with
best throughput ratio `1.1551x` (`+15.5%`) for `full read-bad-node nodes=128
batch=16`; its worst supported throughput ratio was `0.8854x` (`-11.5%`) for
`micro subscription-active-tick nodes=32 batch=8`, still above the strict
comparison floor of `0.85x`. Resource ratios stayed within budget in that run:
max text ratio `0.9927x`, max static RAM ratio `1.0000x`.

`T091` then refreshed `docs/benchmarks/speed-baseline.json` to the accepted
optimized implementation (`generated_at_utc` `2026-06-30T19:51:46.097165+00:00`,
66 supported rows), so future comparisons use the optimized result as the new
baseline. The before/after speed evidence is therefore the `T069`/`T070`
controlled comparison record in Final Validation below, not the refreshed
baseline by itself.
